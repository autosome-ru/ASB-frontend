Motif Concordance
Motif Concordance indicates whether the allelic read imbalance is consistent with the transcription factor Motif Fold Change. Thus, the following values are possible:
  • n/a: No motif available for the transcription factor
  • No hit: Neither Motif Ref P-value nor Motif Alt P-value passes 0.0005 filter
  • Weak concordant: The absolute value of Motif Fold Change is less than 2 and is consistent with allelic read imbalance
  • Weak discordant: The absolute value of Motif Fold Change is less than 2 and is not consistent with allelic read imbalance
  • Concordant: The absolute value of Motif Fold Change is greater or equal than 2 and is consistent with allelic read imbalance
  • Discordant: The absolute value of Motif Fold Change is greater or equal than 2 and is not consistent with allelic read imbalance
